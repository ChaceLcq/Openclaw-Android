# Agenew Product Briefs

Concise fallback briefs for customer-facing introductions. Use extracted files for exact details.

For product-line introductions, group products by category in this order: 4G SoM, 5G SoM, AI SoM, WIFI SoM. Do not group by H-series/M-series unless the customer explicitly asks for series naming.

For broad questions such as "公司现在有哪些产品", "介绍公司产品线", or "有哪些模块", answer by these four categories. Never answer as "H 系列" and "M 系列"; that grouping is obsolete for customer introductions.

## Product Line Categories

### 4G SoM

- H1502BQ, H1502RQ, H1502UQ, H1503BQ, H1503RQ, H1503UQ, H1504TQ: LTE Cat4 Android smart modules with Wi-Fi/BT and GNSS for regional cellular terminal products.
- H1641BP, H1641UP: LTE Cat4 Android smart modules with Wi-Fi/BT, GNSS, and China/India regional positioning.
- H1641RP, H164YP, M1642ZP: LTE Cat7 class Android smart modules for higher 4G bandwidth and regional variants.

### 5G SoM

- M293GO, M318GO: Smart 5G modules with 5G NR, Wi-Fi 6 class wireless, BT5.2, GNSS, Android 13, and richer bandwidth for connected terminals.

### AI SoM

- M3281V: High-performance AI smart-computing SoM with MT8893, Android 15, 48 TOPS, 16GB LPDDR5X + 128GB UFS.
- M328L, M328S: AI smart-computing SoMs with Android 15, 10 TOPS, 8GB LPDDR5X + 128GB UFS for multimedia and edge AI products.

### WIFI SoM

- M274F, M274K: Wi-Fi smart modules with Android 13, Wi-Fi 6/BT5.2, display/camera/audio/peripheral interfaces, for non-cellular connected products.
