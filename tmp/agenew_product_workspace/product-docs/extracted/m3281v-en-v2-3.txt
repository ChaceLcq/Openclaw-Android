M3281V_Smart Module Specification
M3281V
Smart Computing Power Module
M3281V is a smart computing module based on MediaTek’s Octa-core MT8893 4nm processor (1×ARM Cortex-
X4@3.25GHz + 3×ARM Cortex-X4@2.85GHz + 4×ARM Cortex-A720@2.0GHz) launched by AGENEW, with inbuilt ARM
Immortalis-G720 GPU and 7th generation APU 790, equipped with Android 15 operating system. Its powerful performance,
neon engine processing, 2D/3D graphics acceleration capabilities, fully meet the needs of customers for high-speed and
high-computing-power functions in industrial and consumer applications.
M3281V has audio and video input/output interfaces and rich GPIO interfaces, including LCM display modules,
touchscreens, cameras, microphones, speakers, UART, USB, I2C, SPI, PCIe, etc. It supports various audio and video
codecs. The main screen supports up to WQHD (1600×3680)@180fps or 4K (2160×3840)@144fps. AI computing power
reaches 48 TOPS.
M3281V is a SMT module, with a total of 544 pins, all of which are LGA pins, with a size of 42mm×46mm×2.6mm, which
can be embedded in various M2M product applications through the pad, making it widely used in video conferencing, live
streaming, gaming, edge computing, robotics, drones, augmented reality/virtual reality (AR/VR), and other terminal products
and industries.
