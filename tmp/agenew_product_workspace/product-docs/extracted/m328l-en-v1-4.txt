M328L_Smart Module Specification
M328L
Smart Computing Power
Module
Supports Wi-Fi & BT
M328L is a Wi-Fi smart computing module based on MediaTek’s Octa-core MT8371 6nm processor (2×ARM Cortex-
A78@2.2GHz + 6×ARM Cortex-A55@2.0GHz) launched by AGENEW, with inbuilt ARM Mali G57 MC2 GPU and NPU 8.0,
equipped with Android 15 operating system. Its powerful performance, neon engine processing, 2D/3D graphics acceleration
capabilities, fully meet the needs of customers for high-speed and high-computing-power functions in industrial and consumer
applications.
M328L supports 2 × 2 Wi-Fi 6E and BT5.3 near-field wireless communication. In addition, M328L also has audio and
video input/output interfaces and rich GPIO interfaces, including LCM display modules, touchscreens, cameras, microphones,
speakers, UART, USB, I2C, SPI, PCIe, etc. It supports various audio and video codecs. The main screen supports up to
UW5K60(5120×2160) @60fps or 4K60(3840×2160) @60fps. AI computing power reaches 10 TOPS.
M328L is a SMT module, with a total of 455 pins, all of which are LGA pins, with a size of 42mm×46mm×2.6mm, which
can be embedded in various M2M product applications through the pad, making it widely used in video conferencing, live
streaming, gaming, edge computing, robotics, drones, augmented reality/virtual reality (AR/VR), and other terminal products
and industries.
