H150MP M1642ZP_Smart Module Specification
M1642ZP
Multi-network Smart LTE module
Supports Wi-Fi & BT
M1642ZP is a multi-network Smart LTE Cat 7 module based on MediaTek's Octa-core MT8781 (ARM Cortex-A76+ARM
Cortex-A55) processor launched by Wuxi Sunning Technology Co., Ltd, which is equipped with Android 13 operating system.
Its powerful performance, neon engine processing, 2D/3D graphics acceleration processing capabilities, fully meet the needs
of customers for high-speed and multimedia functions in industrial and consumer applications.
M1642ZP supports full coverage of various network standards, Wi-Fi 802.11a/b/g/n/ac and BT5.2 LE short-range
communication. Not only that, M1642ZP also has audio and video I/O interfaces and rich GPIO interfaces, but also supports
a variety of audio and video codecs, and the main screen supports up to 2K. At the same time, the positioning system of
GPS/GLONASS/BDS/Galileo are supported by M1642ZP makes its positioning function fast and accurate.
M1642ZP is a SMT module with 321 pins, including 152 LCC pins and 169 LGA pins. With a size of only 43.0mm ×
44.0mm × 2.6mm, it can be embedded in various M2M products through pads, making it widely used in smart cash registers,
smart POS, tax control machines, security monitoring, vehicle-mounted equipment, high-end information collection equipment,
intelligent robots, smart homes, smart hardware, industrial smart handheld equipment, unmanned aerial vehicles, high-end
police or law enforcement equipment, smart intercom equipment, smart wearing and vending machines.
