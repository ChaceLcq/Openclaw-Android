H1641RP_Smart Module Specification
H1641RP
Multi-network Smart 4G module
Supports Wi-Fi & BT
H1641RP is a multi-network Smart LTE Cat 7 module based on MediaTek's Octa-core (ARM Cortex-A75 + ARM
Cortex-A55) processor MT8786 launched by Wuxi Sunning Technology Co., Ltd, which is equipped with Android 14
operating system. Its powerful performance, neon engine processing, 2D/3D graphics acceleration processing capabilities,
fully meet the needs of customers for high-speed and multimedia functions in industrial and consumer applications.
H1641RP supports full coverage of various network standards, Wi-Fi 802.11a/b/g/n/ac and BT5.0 LE short-range
communication. Not only that, H1641RP also has audio and video I/O interfaces and rich GPIO interfaces, but also
supports a variety of audio and video codecs, and the main screen supports up to FHD+ 2520×1080. At the same time, the
positioning system of GPS/GLONASS/BDS/Galileo are supported by H1641RP makes its positioning function fast and
accurate.
H1641RP is a patch module with 321 pins, including 152 LCC pins and 169 LGA pins. With a size of only
43.0mm×44.0mm×2.6mm, it can be embedded in various M2M products through pads, making it widely used in smart cash
registers, smart POS, tax control machines, security monitoring, vehicle-mounted equipment, high-end information
collection equipment, intelligent robots, smart homes, smart hardware, industrial smart handheld equipment, unmanned
aerial vehicles, high-end police or law enforcement equipment, smart intercom equipment, smart wearing and vending
machines.
