H1504TQ_Smart Module Specification
H1504TQ
Multi-network Smart 4G module
Supports Wi-Fi & BT
H1504TQ is a multi-network Smart LTE Cat 4 module based on MediaTek quad-core ARM Cortex-A53 processor (with a
maximum clock speed of 1.3GHz) launched by Agenew, equipped with Android 10 or 13 operating system. It has powerful
performance, neon engine processing, and 2D/3D graphics acceleration processing capabilities, fully meeting customers'
needs for high speed and multimedia functionality in industrial and consumer applications.
H1504TQ supports full coverage of all network systems, WI-FI 802.11a/b/g/n, and BT4.2 near-field wireless
communication. In addition, the H1504TQ also has audio and video input and output interfaces and rich GPIO interfaces. It
also supports a variety of audio and video codecs. The main screen supports HD+(1440×720)@60fps at most. At the same
time, H1504TQ supports GPS, GLONASS, BDS or Galileo positioning systems, making its positioning function fast and
accurate.
H1504TQ integrates feature rich interfaces (such as LCM, camera, touch screen, microphone, speaker, UART, USB, I2C,
SPI, SDIO, and ADC interfaces), greatly expanding its application in the M2M field, making it widely used in smart cash
registers, smart POS, tax control machines, security monitoring, vehicle equipment, and high-end information Collection
equipment, smart robots, smart homes, smart hardware, industrial smart handheld devices, drones, high-end police or law
enforcement equipment, smart intercom devices, smart wearables, vending machines, logistics cabinets and other equipment
are used in different industries.
