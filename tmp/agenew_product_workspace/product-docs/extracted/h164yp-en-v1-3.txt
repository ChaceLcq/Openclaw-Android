H164YP_Smart Module Specification
H164YP
Multi-network Smart 4G module
Supports Wi-Fi & BT
H164YP is a multi-network Smart LTE Cat 7 module based on MediaTek's Octa-core (Quad-core ARM Cortex-A73+
Quad-core ARM Cortex-A53) processor launched by Wuxi Sunning Technology Co., Ltd, which is equipped with Android 11.0
operating system. Its powerful performance, neon engine processing, 2D/3D graphics acceleration processing capabilities,
fully meet the needs of customers for high-speed and multimedia functions in industrial and consumer applications.
H164YP supports full coverage of various network standards, Wi-Fi 802.11a/b/g/n/ac and BT4.2 LE short-range
communication. Not only that, H164YP also has audio and video I/O interfaces and rich GPIO interfaces, but also supports a
variety of audio and video codecs, and the main screen supports up to FHD 2400×1080. At the same time, the positioning
system of GPS+GLONASS+BDS/Galileo are supported by H164YP makes its positioning function fast and accurate.
H164YP is a chip module with 323 pins, including 152 LCC pins and 171 LGA pins. With a size of only
43.0mm×44.0mm×2.6mm, it can be embedded in various M2M products through pads, making it widely used in smart cash
registers, smart POS, tax control machines, security monitoring, vehicle-mounted equipment, high-end information collection
equipment, intelligent robots, smart homes, smart hardware, industrial smart handheld equipment, unmanned aerial vehicles,
high-end police or law enforcement equipment, smart intercom equipment, smart wearing and vending machines.
